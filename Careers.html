Careers at Oasis Gaming Studios

Game Developer/Programmer

Responsibilities:

  • Write and debug code for game functionality and features.
  • Create and integrate visual and auditory elements.

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in programming languages such as C++, C#, or Java.
  • Experience with game development frameworks and engines.

AI Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Develop artificial intelligence algorithms for non-player character actions.

Requirements:

  • Strong understanding of AI principles and techniques.
  • Experience in game AI development.

Networking Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Work on connectivity aspects for multiplayer games.
  • Ensure smooth gameplay experience across networks.

Requirements:

  • Experience in network programming and protocols.
  • Knowledge of multiplayer game architecture.

VR/AR Developer

Responsibilities:

  • Create games for virtual reality or augmented reality platforms.

Requirements:

  • Experience in VR/AR development.
  • Familiarity with VR/AR hardware and software platforms.

DevOps Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Improve and automate the game development process.
  • Work on the interaction between development and operations.

Requirements:

  • Strong knowledge of software development and deployment processes.
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure automation.

IT Support Specialist

Responsibilities:

  • Maintain and troubleshoot studio's technological equipment.
  • Resolve technical issues and provide technical support.

Requirements:

  • Experience in IT support or related field.
  • Knowledge of computer hardware, software, and networks.

Technical Artist

Responsibilities:

  • Bridge the gap between art and programming in game development.
  • Ensure art assets can be easily integrated without sacrificing vision or efficiency.

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in art and programming tools and workflows.
  • Strong understanding of both artistic and technical aspects of game development.

Narrative Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Craft engaging stories and dialogue for the game.
  • Collaborate with game director and designers to ensure seamless integration.

Requirements:

  • Strong storytelling and writing skills.
  • Experience in narrative design for games or interactive media.

Level Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Design stages or levels within the game.
  • Create settings, challenges, and paths for players.

Requirements:

  • Experience in level design for games.

Monetization Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Work on developing methods to monetize the game.

Requirements:

  • Understanding of game monetization strategies.

User Experience (UX) Designer

Responsibilities:

  • Design the user interface and overall user experience of the game.

Requirements:

  • Experience in UX design for games or software applications.

Game Writer

  • Create all in-game text, including character dialogue, text instructions, and plot development.

  • Experience```html in game writing or related field.

QA Tester

Responsibilities:

  • Test games for bugs and issues.
  • Provide feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Requirements:

  • Experience in game testing.
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
